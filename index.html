Time manipulation is something that is possible right now in very small amounts. It requires someone to either travel really fast, or be closer to a source of gravity. This is possible because of the theory of relativity, stating that time and space aren't two seperate ideas, they are instead one, known as spacetime. This basically means that if someone moves faster through space, they travel slower through time, and vice versa. This leeds to some things that seem simple being really complicated instead, like the satellites that are used for gps locations have to add extra time to their clocks, otherwise it would be way off.
